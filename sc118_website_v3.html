Sunset Cove 118 gives you the best of both worlds — a full condo for stretching out (kitchen, living space, laundry) plus resort perks right outside: the largest pool on Seven Mile Beach, a hot tub, and the Lazy Lizard poolside bar and restaurant.

Step outside and you're at the cove — a calm, protected bay guarded by an enormous rock breakwater. Gentle water, easy swimming, and a perfect spot for kids and floating. It's the easy way to do Seven Mile Beach.

🌅 Why Sunset Cove?

Three reasons guests come back every year

🌊
Calm, protected beach
An enormous rock breakwater stands guard at the edge of the cove — powerful, dramatic, and unforgettable in person. On the inside: gentle water, easy swimming, and a perfect spot for kids and floating.
🏊
Largest pool on SMB
Sunset Cove is home to the largest swimming pool on Seven Mile Beach — sprawling, resort-style, and made for all-day lounging. The Lazy Lizard isn't just a bar: it's a full restaurant with genuinely great food, served poolside all day.
🏡
Condo comfort, resort amenities
Get the best of both worlds: a full condo for stretching out — kitchen, living space, and laundry — plus the resort perks right outside: beach, pool, hot tub, and the Lazy Lizard. It's the easy way to do Seven Mile Beach.
